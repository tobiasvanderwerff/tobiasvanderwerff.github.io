I'm Tobias van der Werff, a robotics and AI engineer based in The Netherlands. I'm the founding engineer at [eternal.ag](https://eternal.ag), where I built the perception system and much of the software for an autonomous tomato harvesting robot. I now lead the engineering team. Before that, I worked on [research](https://github.com/pytorch/ao/pull/983) [paper](https://github.com/tobiasvanderwerff/full-page-handwriting-recognition) [implementations](https://github.com/tobiasvanderwerff/MetaHTR) and low-level [CUDA](https://github.com/pytorch/ao/pull/1147) [performance](https://github.com/pytorch/ao/pull/1092) [optimization](https://github.com/pytorch/ao/pull/942).

You can find my blog [here](/blog/), where I write about topics such as [AI](https://tobiasvanderwerff.com/2024/05/15/cnn-vs-vit.html), [programming](https://tobiasvanderwerff.com/2024/08/01/lindy.html), and [strange hobbies](https://tobiasvanderwerff.com/2025/03/25/dehumidifier.html). I'd describe it as a weird mix of contemplative essays and technical tutorials, with a focus on [practical insights](/2024/06/17/useful-knowledge.html).
